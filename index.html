The Vision Of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Information

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is a country of endless possibilities. Despite facing natural disasters, poverty, and other obstacles, its people have shown incredible resilience, courage, and innovation. I have always believed that if we can unleash the potential of even the poorest individual, we can change the future of our nation. The strength of Bangladesh lies not in its wealth or resources but in its people, their dreams, and their determination to succeed."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date:11/1/2025 Time: 9.55 am

Bangladesh is planning to launch its second satellite, which will focus on Earth observation and disaster management, within the next two to three years. The satellite will help in monitoring natural disasters, managing agricultural resources, and studying environmental changes. A final agreement for the project is expected soon, accelerating its progress. Recently, the country successfully tested a Cube Satellite using a balloon, marking a significant advancement in space technology. These developments showcase Bangladesh’s growing efforts in expanding its space capabilities.
